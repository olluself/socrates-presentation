A problem everyone knows

Modern streaming services offer endless choice, but this creates new challenges.

Choice Paralysis

We spend more time searching than watching, and end up watching nothing at all.

→

The Feeling of Wasted Time

The guilt from hours spent on shows instead of investing time in self-development. **This is the problem we are solving.**

The Solution: Integration, Not Segregation

"Socrates" is not a separate website, but a new layer of analytics and functionality seamlessly built into the familiar Netflix interface. We don't force users to change their habits — we enrich them.

Our system analyzes film scripts to identify key educational themes, skills, and life lessons, then presents this information to the user at the moment of choice.
